🌏 Cultural Heritage Highlights

💃 Dance & Music

Traditional forms like Lavani, Kathak, and classical music reveal India’s deep emotional and artistic spirit.

🎉 Festivals

India celebrates life through colorful festivals like Diwali, Holi, Ganesh Chaturthi, and many regional traditions.

🧵 Handicrafts

Discover the magic of handmade creations like Paithani sarees, Warli art, and Kolhapuri chappals.

🕉️ Spiritual Sites

Temples, shrines, and sacred caves across India reflect centuries of devotion and divine architecture.
